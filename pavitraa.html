Dear Pavitra,

I proposed quickly, but you needed time — time to figure out how a long-distance love could work. For five years, we kept this love going — despite the distance, despite the arguments, despite even the times we broke up — we always returned to each other.

You became my comfort, my safe place — something I never thought I could feel. But life threw us off course, and for reasons I still don't fully understand, you left. I cried, I begged, but you stayed away.

Even now, I'm fighting to keep this love alive — not because I can't live without hope, but because you are my everything. No matter what happens, I won't give up on you. I know I wasn't perfect — I had a bad temper, a rough attitude — but I've changed because of you. I just want you to know that if you choose independence, I respect you completely, but if there's even a chance for us, I'm here. I want to make you happy, today and always.
With all my love 🌹
